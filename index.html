About Me

PhD-level Biostatistician with 12+ years of progressive leadership driving advanced evidence generation, clinical trial design, and data strategy across global pharmaceutical giants (Novartis, Pfizer) and prominent research institutions (CUSAT, MAHE & ICMR). Renowned for bridging mathematical rigor with corporate clinical strategy, specializing in advanced longitudinal modelling in Bayesian and frequentists frameworks, ICH E9(R1) estimands, dealing with Non-propostional hazard scenarios in time-to-event analysis and high-dimensional biomarker modeling. A proven cross-functional partner and mentor who translates complex quantitative data into clear, regulatory-grade medical insights that accelerate drug development portfolios.
